Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology is the branch of medicine that specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders. Our ophthalmologists provide comprehensive eye care, from routine vision exams to complex surgical procedures.

Conditions We Treat

  • Cataracts
  • Glaucoma
  • Diabetic Retinopathy
  • Refractive Errors (Myopia, Hyperopia, Astigmatism)
  • Macular Degeneration
  • Dry Eye Syndrome
Glaucoma: A condition that damages the optic nerve, often due to increased pressure in the eye. Early detection is key to preserving vision.

Eye Exams and Screenings

Regular eye exams are essential for maintaining good vision and catching problems early. We offer vision tests, retinal exams, and eye pressure monitoring as part of our comprehensive eye care services.

Surgical Procedures

Our clinic provides advanced eye surgeries such as cataract removal, LASIK, and treatments for retinal diseases. We use the latest technology to ensure safety and precision.

Pediatric Eye Care

Eye health starts early. Our specialists assess and treat vision issues in children including lazy eye (amblyopia), crossed eyes (strabismus), and nearsightedness.

Vision Care Tips

Protect your eyes by wearing sunglasses, taking breaks from screens, managing chronic health conditions, and avoiding rubbing your eyes. If you wear glasses or contacts, follow proper care instructions.
