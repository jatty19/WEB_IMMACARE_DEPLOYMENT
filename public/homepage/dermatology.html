Dermatology

Dermatology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of skin, hair, and nail conditions. Dermatologists are specialists trained to handle everything from common rashes to complex skin diseases.

Common Skin Conditions We Treat

  • Acne and Acne Scarring
  • Psoriasis and Eczema
  • Skin Infections
  • Hair Loss (Alopecia)
  • Fungal Infections
  • Skin Cancer Screening
Acne: A common skin condition that affects teenagers and adults alike. Treatment includes topical medications, oral antibiotics, or specialized procedures like chemical peels or laser therapy.

Cosmetic Dermatology

In addition to treating skin diseases, dermatology also includes cosmetic procedures to enhance appearance. Services may include Botox, fillers, chemical peels, microneedling, and laser treatments.

Skin Cancer Awareness

Regular skin checks can help detect abnormal moles or lesions early. Dermatologists perform biopsies and recommend treatments for melanoma and other skin cancers.

When to See a Dermatologist

You should see a dermatologist if you notice changes in your skin, develop rashes, persistent acne, hair loss, or nail disorders. Early diagnosis can help prevent complications.

Skin Health Tips

Maintain skin health by using sunscreen daily, staying hydrated, and using gentle skincare products. Avoid excessive sun exposure and consult a dermatologist for personalized advice.
