Urology

Urology focuses on the health of the urinary tract in both men and women, as well as the male reproductive system. Our urologists diagnose and treat a variety of conditions using both medical and surgical interventions.

Conditions We Treat

  • Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs)
  • Kidney Stones
  • Bladder Issues and Incontinence
  • Prostate Enlargement (BPH)
  • Male Fertility Concerns
  • Erectile Dysfunction
Kidney Stones: Hard mineral deposits that form in the kidneys and cause severe pain. We offer pain management, non-invasive treatments, and surgical removal when needed.

Men’s Health

Our clinic offers specialized care for men's health including prostate screening, hormone evaluations, and fertility assessments. Early detection of prostate cancer and other urological conditions is vital.

Bladder Health and Incontinence

Bladder issues can affect daily life and confidence. We provide diagnostic testing and treatment for urinary incontinence, overactive bladder, and other related disorders.

Urologic Surgeries

We perform minimally invasive procedures for kidney stones, prostate issues, and bladder conditions. Laparoscopic and endoscopic techniques are used to reduce recovery time.

When to See a Urologist

You should consult a urologist if you experience frequent urination, blood in urine, pelvic pain, or erectile dysfunction. Early treatment leads to better outcomes.
